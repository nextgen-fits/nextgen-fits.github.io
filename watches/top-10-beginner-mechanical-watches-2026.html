01. TIME100 Navigator Automatic Skeleton

Movement: Automatic (Miyota-based Architecture)
Case Diameter: 42mm
Water Resistance: 50m
Features: Full Skeleton Dial, Sun/Moon Phase Display

If you want a watch that commands attention from across the room without draining your bank account, the TIME100 Navigator is an absolute masterclass in visual showmanship. Featuring a deeply layered, fully skeletonized dial architecture, this timepiece exposes the rhythmic beating heart of its automatic movement, complete with a beautifully integrated rolling sun/moon phase indicator at the 6 o'clock position.

Unlike standard budget open-heart watches that look cheap under inspection, the finishing here plays incredibly well with directional lighting. It bridges the gap between pure industrial cyberpunk aesthetics and classical watchmaking heritage, making it a stellar entry-level centerpiece for any collector.
